# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful community.

- Be kind and professional.
- Assume good intent and seek to understand before responding.
- No harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct.
- Use inclusive language and provide constructive feedback.

Enforcement: Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation that violates this policy. For severe or repeated violations, report privately via the contact in `SECURITY.md`.

Adapted from the Contributor Covenant (shortened).
